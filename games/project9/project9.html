About the Project

A very basic isometric style shooter test project. Pixel art is time consuming and requires a lot of skill, so I wanted to streamline that process a bit and make use of the skills I already have, so I modeled and animated the character models in Blender and rendered them into spritesheet animations to use in Godot. I found that this workflow was fast, flexible and produced good results with minimal effort. This way of making 2D art is also much more consistent as the models' proportions stay the same throughout every frame by default. Small changes can also be done afterwards and all that is needed is to re-render, as opposed to having to manually draw every frame again by hand. Experimented with NPC speech bubbles as well.
