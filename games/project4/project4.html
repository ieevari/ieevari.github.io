OnkiOps

A top-down pixel-art fishing game.

About the Project

My attempt at making a fishing simulator. This project helped me learn more about procedural generation which, for me personally, is one of the most interesting areas of game dev. Every asset except for the sounds of the oars splashing in water is made by me. I wanted to simulate real fishing as much as possible, so I made all sorts of variables to control the chance and effectiveness of catching a fish. Each species has a different preference and each lure has it's own strengths.
